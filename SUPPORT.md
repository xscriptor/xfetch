<h2 id="before-opening-an-issue" align="center">Before Opening an Issue</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Confirm your platform and version (Linux/macOS/Windows).</li>
  <li>Share your active config and layout file (remove sensitive details if any).</li>
  <li>Include command output and reproduction steps.</li>
  <li>If visual output differs, attach screenshots when possible.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="response-expectations" align="center">Response Expectations</h2>

<p>
  Maintainers triage reports based on impact and reproducibility. Clear, minimal reproductions are resolved faster.
</p>
