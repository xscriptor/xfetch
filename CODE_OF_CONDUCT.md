<h1 align="center">Code of Conduct</h1>

<p align="center">
  Xfetch contributors and users are expected to collaborate in a respectful, inclusive, and professional way.
</p>

<hr />

<h2 id="our-standards" align="center">Our Standards</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Communicate with respect and technical clarity.</li>
  <li>Provide constructive feedback on code, docs, and design decisions.</li>
  <li>Welcome contributors across platforms and experience levels.</li>
  <li>Keep discussions focused on improving Xfetch for everyone.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="unacceptable-behavior" align="center">Unacceptable Behavior</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.</li>
  <li>Personal attacks, mockery, or hostile escalation.</li>
  <li>Deliberate disruption of project discussions or reviews.</li>
  <li>Publishing private information without permission.</li>
</ul>

<h2 id="reporting" align="center">Reporting</h2>

<p>
  Report incidents to <a href="mailto:x@xscriptor.com">x@xscriptor.com</a>. Reports are treated confidentially.
</p>

<hr />

<p align="center"><b>Thank you for helping maintain a healthy open-source community.</b></p>
